The 22 deity crystals
Twenty-two premium crystals were created by deity in ancient times. Each one carries unique power. Only two have been named so far in Red-Eye.
Agastha Crystal
The soul spiritual stone. Signifies love in its purest concentrated form. Responds to and grows with genuine love. Created during the 3rd Seven Nation War. Creator officially unknown — but the Entity claims to have made it. One is planted inside Sunny's body.

Known abilities: enhances physical and mental capacity — manipulates environment (not full dimension) — accelerates wound healing — power of influence — required ingredient for ascending to Deity classification — and more yet unrevealed.

Notable rare materials
Aurichalcum
Known as golden copper or mountain copper. Extremely rare metal. Found beneath Milt Town — land the kingdom dismissed as worthless. Its discovery triggered the Milt Town massacre and set the king on his decade-long hunt for the Agastha Crystal.

Amethyst crystal necklace
Given to Emma by the Entity during Sunny's absence. Purple quartz associated with peace, protection, and spiritual wisdom. Activates crown and third-eye chakras. Enhances intuition and emotional balance. Extremely rare — documented only in King Luthur's ancient writings.

Locations
Riverend
Sunny's hometown — outcast settlement
A poor town where Sunny's family lives as outcasts. Official tax rate is 15% for residents — but outcasts are charged whatever the deputy decides, often reaching 50% or more. Maya's exceptional spice craft is systematically drained by this corruption. The Emperion's Army nominally protects the region but enforcement is selective and often cruel.
Outcast zoneCorrupt taxationSunny's origin
Xianzhu
Noble town — elite class
A town of rich and noble houses where high-class people live. One month's journey by cart from Riverend. Casser — top commander of the Emperion's Army — is based here. Sunny has only heard of it in stories. The gap between Riverend and Xianzhu represents the world's class divide in physical form.
Noble classCasser's base
Borderland Town
Nova's kingdom — mountain fortress
Surrounded entirely by mountains so high they disappear into clouds — making the town nearly impossible to conquer from outside. Ruled by Nova, goddess of lightning and creator of the Alasta Crystal. Sara and Naya's home. Nova once fought an unknown god for four days straight and lost — the mountains still bear the scars. The unknown god returned her kingdom in exchange for support during the Trial of Heaven in the Methodic Era.
Nova's domainMountain fortressSara's origin
Milt Town
Vanished settlement — site of the massacre
Once a poor village dismissed as worthless land. The Entity arrived here — his presence caused the king's father to lose grip on the town. Aurichalcum was discovered beneath it. The king's father ordered a public assault on its women as punishment for defiance. The Entity killed 50 soldiers and the king's father in response. Five days later, every trace of the town — every person, every structure — had completely vanished. The king has been searching for this location and the Agastha Crystal ever since.

The fog-covered forest Sunny ran through on the night of the deal. Site of Viktor and Amantha's attack. Where the Entity first appeared. Contains the waterfall where Sunny unlocked Technique 1. The king's wolves tracked the Agastha Crystal signal here. The rain erased the trail. The forest holds more secrets than anyone currently understands.

World eras
Methodic Era
The era during which the Trial of Heaven took place. Nova fought the unknown god in this period and lost — surrendering her kingdom, then receiving it back in exchange for support during the Trial. Ancient history by the time Red-Eye begins.
Agonite Era — current
The Era of Shadows and Corruption. A name chosen by people who watched something shift in the world and stopped pretending otherwise. The era in which Red-Eye takes place. Multiple factions compete for power. Ancient forces resurface. A ten-year-old outcast boy carries something that could change everything.
Key historical events
1
Seven Nation War — Multiple wars referenced. Sunny's father died in one. The Agastha Crystal was created during the 3rd Seven Nation War.
2
Trial of Heaven — Occurred during the Methodic Era. Nova agreed to support it in exchange for having her kingdom returned by the unknown god.
3
Milt Town massacre — 10 years before Red-Eye begins. The Entity killed 50 soldiers and the king's father. Milt Town vanished entirely five days later.
4
Alice's disappearance — A Type 1 God of extraordinary power. Believed by some to have touched Type 0. Vanished under unknown circumstances. Her bracelet now carried by the Entity.
5
King Luthur's writings — Ancient chronicles documenting the Eight Categories and early world history. Sunny's father owned a copy. The only source that describes Alice's bracelet.
